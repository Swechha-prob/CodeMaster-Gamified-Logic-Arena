ℹ️ About

🎓 CodeMaster Arena

An interactive learning platform designed to master core programming concepts through gamified quizzes and hands-on challenges.

🎯 Our Mission

CodeMaster Arena is built with one core mission: to empower learners at Chitkara University and beyond to master programming fundamentals through interactive, engaging challenges. We believe that the best way to learn programming is through active practice, immediate feedback, and gamification that keeps learners motivated.

Our platform combines theoretical knowledge with practical problem-solving to create a comprehensive learning experience that prepares students for real-world development challenges.
